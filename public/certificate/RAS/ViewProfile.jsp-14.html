RAS Center Saud Bahwan Automotive LLC Saud Bahwan Automotive LLC (Marmul Workshop inside PDO)

SAUD BAHWAN AUTOMOTIVE LLC Saud Bahwan Automotive LLC (SBA) is a part of Saud Bahwan Group (SBG), a leading business group in the Sultanate of Oman and is headed by Sheikh Mohammed Saud Bahwan as Chairman. SBA’s mission is to get world-class products in to Oman and back it up with world class after sales support.

Each operation of SBA is independently structured to give the Business and the Brand the requisite focus. With a long-term perspective, SBA has made investments in infrastructure particularly in the After Sales support area of Spare Parts and Service all across the country, which are continuously strengthened with the core objective of enhancing customer satisfaction. SBA (earlier called Suhail and Saud Bahwan LLC) began its operations in 1975 when the Historic Distributor agreement was signed with Toyota Motor Corporation for marketing and selling Toyota in Oman.

Since then, SBA has grown by adding a host of reputed brands like Toyota, Lexus, Daihatsu, Hino, Yokohama etc. SBA is diversified and has the following key businesses: • Cars & Light Commercial Vehicles - Toyota, Lexus, Daihatsu • Heavy Vehicles - Hino • Tyres and Batteries- Yokohama, Furukawa etc..

• Construction Equipment - Bomag, Sullair, Wirtgen, Vermeer etc.. • Special Equipment Division – Toyota Forklifts, Johnston Sweeper, etc.
